# Broad Symbolic Analysis Protocol

Use this file when "feng shui" means more than spatial layout: 吉凶, 运势, 趋吉避凶, 五行取象, a person's 生平, a business situation, 金融 or financial decision, an event omen, or a cross-domain question that needs traditional Chinese symbolic reasoning.

## Core Rule

In broad use, feng shui is a language for 观气, 取象, 辨势, 择时, 调和, and 趋吉避凶. It can read tendencies, support, leakage, timing, balance, and risk posture across many domains, not only rooms and land.

Always keep three layers separate:

1. **Reality layer**: facts, constraints, domain evidence, safety, law, finance, health, relationships, and user agency.
2. **Symbolic layer**: yin-yang, 五行, 八卦, 干支, form/flow, timing, support, leakage, sha qi, ji/xiong.
3. **Action layer**: low-risk adjustments that improve real conditions and symbolically seek favorable qi.

Do not turn symbolic reading into deterministic fate, guaranteed wealth, medical diagnosis, legal advice, or market prediction.

## Seven-Step Protocol

| Step | Chinese frame | Practical question | Output |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | 定域 | What domain and stakes are involved? | Space, finance, career, person, event, relationship, product, business, or mixed |
| 2 | 观气 | Where is movement, pressure, stagnation, exposure, or support? | Flow map |
| 3 | 取象 | Which symbols fit the evidence? | Yin-yang, 五行, 八卦, timing, form, gan-zhi scaffold |
| 4 | 辨势 | Is the pattern rising, leaking, blocked, overheated, hidden, or unsupported? | Risk/opportunity pattern |
| 5 | 断吉凶 | Under what conditions is this favorable, mixed, or risky? | Conditional ji/xiong assessment |
| 6 | 化解 | What can be adjusted safely and reversibly? | Practical and symbolic remedies |
| 7 | 复核 | What real-world evidence would confirm or reject the reading? | Missing inputs and next checks |

Use this protocol even for quick answers, but compress it into a few sentences when the user does not need a full report.

## Symbolic Lenses

### Yin-Yang

- Yang: exposure, speed, heat, action, publicity, volatility, performance.
- Yin: concealment, rest, reserves, privacy, depth, waiting, consolidation.
- Favorability comes from fit: a launch may need yang; recovery may need yin. Excess creates risk.

### Five Phases

Use 五行 as process dynamics:

| Phase | General image | Cross-domain reading |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Wood | growth and direction | learning, expansion, pipeline, ambition, early-stage development |
| Fire | visibility and heat | launch, marketing, speculation, attention, leadership, emotion |
| Earth | center and containment | reserves, trust, routine, governance, durability, caregiving |
| Metal | boundary and refinement | rules, pruning, audit, precision, contracts, risk controls |
| Water | flow and depth | liquidity, research, networks, adaptability, information, uncertainty |

### Bagua

Use 八卦 as a symbolic map only when it helps organize the question. Do not force every topic into all eight trigrams.

| Trigram | Useful image | Example domains |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Qian | authority, strategy, heaven | leadership, father/mentor, governance |
| Kun | support, receptivity, earth | operations, family support, capacity |
| Zhen | movement, shock, thunder | sudden events, launch, initiative |
| Xun | penetration, wind, influence | communication, brand spread, negotiation |
| Kan | danger, water, depth | finance liquidity, uncertainty, research |
| Li | clarity, fire, visibility | marketing, reputation, attention |
| Gen | stillness, mountain | boundaries, pause, study, property |
| Dui | joy, lake, exchange | sales, social tone, pleasure, speech |

### Form and Flow Beyond Space

When there is no floor plan, treat "form" as the structure of the situation:

- **Mountain/backing**: support, credentials, savings, allies, documentation.
- **Ming tang/open hall**: room for opportunity, runway, clean decision space.
- **Water/flow**: cash, information, users, communication, attention.
- **Sha qi**: sharp deadlines, conflict, debt, legal exposure, unsafe behavior, public pressure.
- **Leakage**: money, focus, time, trust, reputation, or energy draining away.

## Ji/Xiong Assessment

Use 吉凶 as conditional language:

| Reading | Conditions | Recommended posture |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 吉 | Supported, timely, balanced, contained, reversible | Proceed with checks and measured activation |
| 凶 | Unsupported, unsafe, rushed, leaking, conflicted, overexposed | Delay, reduce scale, add protection, or avoid |
| 半吉半凶 | Real upside and visible risk coexist | Split the decision, test small, add controls |
| 可化 | Risk can be reduced through timing, support, containment, or simplification | Remedy first, reassess after change |

Never call a person, birth, home, investment, illness, or relationship "doomed."

## Life Pattern Reading

For a person's 生平, read phases and recurring patterns instead of fixed destiny:

- **Early pattern**: growth, family support, education, movement, constraints.
- **Middle pattern**: career visibility, responsibility, partnership, resource flow.
- **Later pattern**: consolidation, legacy, health-adjacent environment, support network.
- **Repeated issue**: leakage, overfire, stagnation, weak backing, harsh metal, drifting water, overgrown wood.

If the user provides birth data, use only the deterministic helpers actually present in this skill, such as ming gua or a Gregorian-year ganzhi scaffold. State clearly that this is not a complete 八字, 紫微, 奇门, 六壬, or almanac calculation.

## Finance and Decision Reading

For finance, business, or major decisions:

1. Start with the native discipline: valuation, cash flow, risk tolerance, liquidity, rules, law, tax, and time horizon.
2. Translate symbolically:
   - Water: liquidity, information, adaptability.
   - Wood: growth thesis and compounding.
   - Fire: market heat, attention, hype, momentum.
   - Earth: reserves, ballast, durable protection.
   - Metal: position sizing, stop/review rules, discipline.
3. Judge 吉凶 conditionally:
   - Favorable when the real thesis is sound, downside is contained, liquidity is adequate, and the user is not chasing fire.
   - Risky when the action is overexposed, under-researched, concentrated, emotionally driven, or lacks exit rules.
4. Give controls: smaller position, waiting period, written thesis, review date, diversification, or professional advice.

Never answer "buy/sell" purely from feng shui symbolism.

## Output Pattern

For substantial broad symbolic readings, use:

1. **Scope and method**: domain, stakes, and symbolic lenses used.
2. **Reality layer**: facts and constraints before symbolism.
3. **Symbolic analysis protocol**: brief 观气 / 取象 / 辨势 / 吉凶 summary.
4. **Ji/xiong assessment**: favorable conditions, risk conditions, mixed areas.
5. **Actions for 趋吉避凶**: practical and symbolic adjustments.
6. **Missing inputs and limits**: what would change confidence.

## Boundaries

- High-stakes finance, medical, legal, safety, relationship, pregnancy, death, disaster, or market questions require `ethics-and-limits.md`.
- Use specialized adapters when present: finance, business, brand, career, product, learning, wellbeing, relationship, legal-adjacent, and life/omen.
- Do not invent birth charts, almanac attributes, market data, medical facts, legal facts, or private biographical details.
- A strong symbolic pattern still remains an interpretation, not proof.
