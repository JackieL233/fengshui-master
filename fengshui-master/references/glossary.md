# Glossary

Use this file when the user asks for terms, translations, or quick conceptual clarification.

## Core Terms

| Term | Chinese | Meaning |
| --- | --- | --- |
| feng shui | 风水 | wind-water; traditional spatial practice for qi and environment |
| qi | 气 | vital influence, breath, movement, or environmental quality |
| yin-yang | 阴阳 | relational polarity: still/active, dark/bright, inward/outward |
| wuxing | 五行 | five phases: wood, fire, earth, metal, water |
| bagua | 八卦 | eight trigrams used as symbolic/directional categories |
| luopan | 罗盘 | feng shui compass with multiple interpretive rings |
| ming tang | 明堂 | bright hall; open gathering space before a site or entrance |
| sha qi | 煞气 | harsh, cutting, rushing, oppressive, or destabilizing influence |
| sheng qi | 生气 | growing or life-supporting qi; also a ba zhai favorable direction |
| xing shi | 形势 | form and configuration; landform or shape-force reading |
| li qi | 理气 | patterning qi through direction, time, and formula |
| sitting | 坐 | rear or backing direction of building/seat/bed |
| facing | 向 | active, open, or front direction |
| 24 mountains | 二十四山 | 24 compass sectors of 15 degrees each |
| heavenly stems | 天干 | ten calendrical stems |
| earthly branches | 地支 | twelve calendrical branches |
| san he | 三合 | school family emphasizing form, water, branches, and directional combinations |
| san yuan | 三元 | school family emphasizing time cycles and spatial qi |
| xuan kong | 玄空 | "mysterious void"; often used for flying-star time-space methods |
| fei xing | 飞星 | flying stars; nine-star charting method |
| ba zhai | 八宅 | eight mansions; personal/house direction method |
| ming gua | 命卦 | personal gua number in eight mansions |
| li chun | 立春 | start of spring solar term, often used as a year boundary in some calculations |
| tai sui | 太岁 | year deity concept; used in annual timing cautions |
| sui po | 岁破 | year-breaker direction opposite tai sui in some annual methods |
| san sha | 三煞 | three killings; annual directional caution in some systems |

## Favorable and Unfavorable Ba Zhai Terms

| Term | Common translation | Use |
| --- | --- | --- |
| sheng qi | life-generating | vitality, growth, opportunity |
| tian yi | heavenly doctor | support, recovery, helpful people |
| yan nian | longevity | harmony, relationships, endurance |
| fu wei | stability | calm, self-cultivation, steadiness |
| jue ming | life-ending | severe challenge in symbolic ranking |
| wu gui | five ghosts | disturbance, volatility |
| liu sha | six killings | conflict, entanglement |
| huo hai | mishaps | minor trouble, friction |

Use these as symbolic categories, not literal predictions.

## Common Translation Cautions

- Qi is broader than "energy"; use "qi" and explain context.
- Wuxing are "phases" or "processes", not just physical elements.
- Sha qi should usually be translated into observable pressure, hazard, or discomfort.
- A "cure" is better phrased as an adjustment or remedy.
- "Auspicious" means favorable within a method, not guaranteed success.
